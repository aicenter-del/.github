## 🔬 4 Main Research Pillars & Active Projects

Our innovations are focused on four strategic domains to deliver tangible impact for both society and industry:

### 🎓 1. AI for Education and STEM Learning
Improving the quality of mathematics and science education through adaptive and personalized AI technology.
* **AI Math Tutor:** An AI-based tutoring system for elementary and middle school mathematics using the Gasing approach (*In Development*).
* **Learning Analytics Dashboard:** An early detection platform for student learning difficulties based on assessment data (*Initial Research*).
* **Augmented Assessment:** An adaptive testing system utilizing NLP and computer vision for formative evaluation (*Planning*).

### 🌅 2. AI for Local Culture-Based Tourism
Integrating artificial intelligence with the cultural wealth of Lake Toba to create personalized and sustainable tourism experiences.
* **EVA-Del (Toba Virtual Tourism Assistant):** A fine-tuned local LLM designed to answer queries regarding tourism, culture, and services in Lake Toba (*Beta Testing*).
* **Personalized Tourism Recommendation System:** A recommendation algorithm based on user preferences, seasonality, and local context (*In Development*).
* **Toba Digital Culture Museum:** Semantic mapping and digital archiving of Toba Batak cultural artifacts with AI-powered search (*Planning*).

### 🏥 3. AI for Public Health and Government
Supporting data-driven decision-making for healthcare services and responsive, ethical public governance (adhering to Indonesia's Personal Data Protection Law **UU PDP No. 27/2022**).
* **Health Load Predictor:** A prediction model for hospital loads and early detection of disease outbreak risks (*Model Validation*).
* **Fraud Detection System:** Classification and NLP tools to detect anomalies in insurance claims and public services (*Planning*).

### 🌾 4. AI for Precision Agriculture & Bioinformatics
Leveraging AI to unlock local genetic potential and enhance sustainable agricultural productivity.
* **Genomic Diversity Analyzer:** NGS data analysis to identify superior genes and genetic diversity in local crops (*Initial Research*).
* **Plant Disease Classifier:** A computer vision model for the early detection of plant diseases based on leaf imagery (*Data Collection*).

## 📦 Open Datasets & Research Resources
We are committed to open science by providing public data assets:
* [**Toba Culture Dataset (Semantic):**](https://aicenter.del.ac.id/datasets/budaya-toba) Metadata of artifacts and traditional narratives of the Toba Batak people (JSON/CSV, CC-BY 4.0).
* [**Local Plant Imagery Dataset:**](https://aicenter.del.ac.id/datasets/citra-tanaman) A collection of local plant leaf images with disease patterns for training computer vision models (CC-BY-NC 4.0).
* [**AI Ethics Guidelines & Templates:**](https://aicenter.del.ac.id/toolkit/etika-ai) AI Ethics Guidelines for Researchers & Technical Checklists for AI Model Validation prior to deployment.
